Blockchains

Blockchain is a database system invented to store information, after having entered database, the information will be stored in different blocks, which are all connected to create a chain. The information in these blocks cannot be adjusted after entered the database, but the blocks can be extended by adding another block. Blockchains are automated, managed by several members and believed to be safe and reliable. However, they usually become the favorite target of hackers because of the classified information they store, most of which is money related. To sum up, blockchains can be described as an online server that store information.

learn more

How they can determine the future?

Having said so, it is not the ability to store data of blockchains that human beings pay attention to, it is the ability to solve the trusting issues that people used to have when doing transaction or trading. In the past, when two people were in a financial deal, there would always be a person that played an intermediary role. However, this had become a dilemma since the people that involved directly in the deal always had to face the trusting issues: they had to neither trust their customers/sellers or the moderators. A lot of issues could happen, such as the moderator turned back on both or the moderator being one-sided. In blockchains, people trust nobody but the special algorithms that were developed to guarantee their transactions and secure their personal information. All the information is encrypted to ensure that transaction using blockchains is safe and secure. Blockchains have now being used by a lot of technology-based organizations and companies, especially by companies whose services are online, namely Amazon, Alibaba, E-bay. These companies are operated in the global scale and they use blockchains to protect the rights of both customers as well as service providers.

Cryptocurrency

Like any currencies in use at the present, cryptocurrency is a kind of money, however, this money does not exist but distributed throughout the cloud network. Cryptocurrency is based on the algorithms of blockchains. In order to collect crypto tokens, local machines have to perform calculations to complete blocks of verified transaction in the blockchains. This action is referred as mining, and crypto tokens are the rewards for investors.

How they can determine the future?

The most popular of its kind is Bitcoin. Even though Bitcoin was debut long ago, around 2010, the occasion that Bitcoin shocked the world was when its price reached well over 20000 US dollars, and many stores in the world started to accept bitcoin as a payment method. Specifically, a man was able to purchase a Lamborghini using bitcoins in the United States, or many well-known brands such as Louis Vuitton, Gucci allowed customers to purchase their products using bitcoins and other cryptocurrencies. Saying cryptocurrency is the currency of the future of is not exaggerating. The characteristics of cryptocurrency allows individuals from all countries with internet access to perform transaction globally, equally, and limitlessly, because the transactions cannot be tracked or controlled by any governments or organizations, which means no regulations can be applied. Cryptocurrency is a non-border currency and has huge potential to become the main type of currency in the global. It is the future, but personally speaking, in 5 year-time, cryptocurrency may not be widely accepted by most individuals but IT professionals and tech-savvy people. This is mainly because of the conservative mindset of the current generation, the generation who holds a large amount of the world assets. For about hundreds of years, assets were supposed to be existing and could be managed by their owners. Consequently, it will be challenging for the present generation to change their mindset and invest in cryptocurrencies, which cannot be seen or touched, despite their valuable personalities and the huge potential that they carry. Moreover, Bitcoin, or cryptocurrencies in general, do not hold their own values. The value of cryptocurrency is still calculated based on the value of the world’s current money such as US dollar or British pound, which makes cryptocurrency seems like an online version of those currencies, or even a method for global traders to evade taxations, which completely ruins the essence of cryptocurrency.

Adaptive cruise control - the beginning of a new era?

Cars nowadays can partly drive themselves thanks to the autopilot systems. Car manufacturers usually call them adaptive cruise control. In the past, all cruise control could do is to keep the car at a chosen speed so the drivers can let their foot off the pedals, however the drivers were still responsible for braking and lane changing. Nowadays, Adaptive Cruise Control is way more advanced as they can intervene in all the driving systems including accelerating, braking, and steering. Radars inside the vehicles can detect the roads infrastructure (lanes, signs) so they can keep the car in lane and under the speed limit. Distance radar are responsible for controlling the distance between it and the car in front, thus maintaining safe distance. This can be described as a background for manufacturers to add more features to the autopilot system, which will gradually add on to the process of producing autonomous vehicles.

Levels of self-driving vehicles

Six levels of automation

No Automation

Drivers are in fully control of the vehicles

Driver Assisstance

Vehicles assisst drivers on some specific tasks (speed maintaining) but drivers are still responsible for controlling the vehicles

Partial Automation

Vehicles can perform different tasks and intervene in the vehicles' system to actively keep the vehicles safe (emergency braking, lane keeping)

Conditional Automation

Vehicles can perform diffucult tasks such as lane changing, parking under the orders of owners

High automation

Vehicles can mostly operate without drivers' action, but still require supervision of drivers

Full Automation

Vehicles can operate themselves without any intervention of humans
