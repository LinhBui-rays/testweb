Detailing Tunnel

The IT Industry is so wide and limitless that it allows us to come up with a lot of ideas so that we can improve our livesâ€™ quality and convenience.  As a cars and motors lovers, I am really into the car detailing industry, in which you perform different tasks to maintain your vehicles at its very best conditions. Unfortunately, car washing in Vietnam does not offer adequate quality as well as consumes a lot of time and labor works. This is the reason why I came up with the idea of building an automated car wash system to replace the traditional method of washing our cars.

That is not to mention the fact that the weather in our country is unpredictable with rainy and sunny days alternately, and the facts that our roads are often packed with dirt and muds, thus making car washing an action that needs to be performed on a weekly basis.

Flexibility

The system is able to detect the car size, models, and record data on how much water, foam, liquid was used after the washing process, from that determine different prices for different cars. Service quality can also be flexible due to the wish of customers, since expensive cars with high quality paintwork often requires better care.

Data analytics

Types of car, brands, the time that people usually bring their cars in for service, the relation between weather conditions and number of cars washed will all be collected and analyzed. This is not only used to optimizing qualityâ€™s service but also improving efficiency and profits.

Tunnel Design

This system will have the design similarly to a tunnel. The tunnel will be divided into 2 parts and the first part will be responsible for washing the car.

Washing and Polishing

At these stage, the vehicles will be cleaned using pressured water, foam, then polishing to achieve the best results.  The whole process would take 10 to 12 minutes depending on vehicles' size.

Washing and Polishing

Enhance the look of your vehicles with the best services

Snow Foam

There will be foam canons attached to all sides of the tunnel to cover the whole car in foam. This stage will soften all the dirt on the carâ€™s surface to make the washing more efficient and would take about 3 minutes.

Cleaning

Water will be sprayed at high pressure to wash all the foam and dirt away. Warm water will be used instead of cold water since it would help spray the cars more effectively and soften the water to prevent any possible damages to the paintwork. 

Polishing

 Sealant, a liquid that create a protective layer on top the paintwork, will be sprayed on the surface of the vehicles, thus making the serviced vehicles shining and keep them clean for a longer period.

Extended features

What seperate us from the conventional wasing method

The water pump will have travel distance of 20 to 30 centimeters (left and right, up, and down) to make sure all parts are cleaned properly. Cameras will be attached in the room to control the quality of the washing process, so if the camera detects dirt on the surface of the car after the process, those parts will be washed again. Water pressures can also vary unlike the traditional washing method. The lower part of the car and the wheels, which is usually dirtier, can be washed with higher pressure compared to the upper part. Sensors inside the room can also detect the size of cars to make sure that the pump is always 20 to 30 centimeters away from the car surface so washing is performed at the highest efficiency and ensure that the paintwork is not damaged by the high pressure.

Drying and Interior Cleaning

Final steps to achieve perfection

Drying

the vehicle is transferred to the second room for drying. Air flows will be used to dry to even the smallest parts of the vehicles, and warm airflows will be used to evaporate water more efficiently compared to drying cars with towels. This stage takes about 3 minutes regardless of the vehicle size. The whole tunnel process takes about 10 to 12 minutes maximum.

Interior Cleaning

When the vehicle is out, it will be moved to the interior cleaning bay if the owners request, if not, they can drive out immediately since the interior is often cleaner compared to the exterior of the vehicles. Interior cleaning is still performed by humans since machines cannot perform this service yet.

The use of data

The camera would detect registration number of the vehicles and store them in a database. All the information of customers will be recorded and analyzed for improving customer service and system efficiency (allocating the right time for system maintenance, for example). This information is also used for improving service, detect frequent customers to bring out the right membership program for customers.
