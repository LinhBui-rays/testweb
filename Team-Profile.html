The innovators

I believe the world, especially the IT industry, is always moving forwards. Creativity, innovation, and inspiration are significant driving forces that boost the advancement of technology. Our team name, The Innovators, somewhat describe our passion and desire to bring the world forward and explore various aspects of the IT industry.

Our team members include Lam Tin Dieu, Truong Cao Ba Tri (Jacob), Nguyen Ha Dieu Anh (Dian), and me. We formed this team mostly base on our impressions after getting to know each other for a few weeks, but this team turns out to be my dream team.

Truong Cao Ba Tri (Jacob)

Ba Tri is one of the most talented and intellectual students I have ever met. He excels in many areas, namely social networking, academic, curriculum activities, also I noticed that he has many characteristics of a good team leader. Having had a well-built background, he is always confident and willing to propose his ideas. I am fortunate to have the chance to work with him in the final projects as he is energetic, open-minded would act as a source of inspiration and motivation for the whole team.

Lam Tin Dieu

Dieu is kind, open-minded and very sociable. After 5 weeks studying together in two different subjects, I knew we could cooperate with each other since we were able to communicate and exchange ideas efficiently. Base on his test results, he has good logical thinking and is capable of solving complicated problems. Additionally, Dieu is well-organized and responsible, he always attempted to complete all the assessments on time, which are the perfect personalities that I would like to see in my teammates.

Regardless of characteristics or working style, it is important to me that my team completes all the tasks on time and members should have a sense of responsibility. This would be a major factor that determines the final outcomes of the group projects. Personality though, should also be taken into consideration, as cooperating is not just about responsibility and finishing everything before deadlines, it is also about mutual understanding, the ability to work with each other so all the team could contribute to the project and avoid any possible errors. Conflicts and arguments are inevitable when it comes to teamwork, however it is the way that the whole team overcomes the conflicts and keep the group moving forward that matters. Personally speaking, I am an extroverted person, and as my personality type suggests, I am very sociable so I can work with any personality types. Still, I preferred to work with teammates that are opening, charming, supportive, and willing to contribute their ideas. I believe listening and contributing ideas and giving feedbacks are the keys to any team’s success.
